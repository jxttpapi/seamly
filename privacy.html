Privacy Policy

How Seamly handles your data — short and simple.

The short version: Seamly does not collect personal data, does not use analytics, and requires no account. Your streaming activity stays entirely on your device.

Overview

Seamly is a media streaming app for iOS. This policy explains what the app accesses, why, and what happens to that information.

Seamly is designed to work entirely on your device. No personal data is sent to external servers.

Device permissions

🌐
Network Access — Required
Used to stream video and audio from URLs and links you provide. Seamly only connects to sources you explicitly open.
🔊
Background Audio — Required
Allows audio to continue playing when the app is in the background or your screen is locked. This is a core feature of Seamly.

What we don't collect

Seamly does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

Personal information
Viewing history
Location data
Device identifiers
Usage analytics
Crash reports
Advertising IDs
Any tracking data

Content & streaming

Seamly does not host, store, or cache any media content. When you play a stream, your device connects directly to the source URL. Seamly has no knowledge of or access to the content you stream.

Third-party services

Seamly does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. No data is shared with any third party under any circumstances.

Children's privacy

Seamly does not collect any information from any user, including children under 13. The app fully complies with COPPA and similar regulations because no data collection occurs at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the effective date below will change. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
