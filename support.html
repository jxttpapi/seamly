Frequently Asked Questions

Why won't some videos play?
Some platforms restrict playback outside their official apps. This is a limitation set by the content provider, not Seamly.
Does Seamly store or host videos?
No. Seamly does not host, store, or cache any content. It simply plays streams you provide — your data stays between you and the source.
Background playback isn't working — what do I do?
Make sure the stream supports audio output and that your device's background app refresh is enabled. Some streams are video-only and don't support audio in the background.
What stream formats does Seamly support?
Seamly supports HLS (.m3u8), direct HTTP video/audio URLs, and M3U playlist links. If you have a compatible link, paste it in and you're ready to go.
Is Seamly free to use?
Yes, Seamly is free to download on the App Store. Some features may be available as optional upgrades in the future.
